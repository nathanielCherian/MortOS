Commands:
add, subtract, multiply, divide: perform the desired operation on two numbers
pythag: find the hypotenuse of a right triangle give 2 legs
cls: clear screen
wave: cool wave animation!
boom: cool circle animation!
base: cool animation
launch: physics based animation; shows path of a ball shot out of a cannon
im: image to ascii converter
rfile: read file (verified user exclusive)
wfile: write to file (verified user exclusive)
fibonacci: gives a term of the fibonacci sequence
pas: prints out pascals triangle as a pyramid

Present:
$ cd present
$ sh present.sh
